About My WatchList

My WatchList is a Android application that allows you to maintain a list of your favorite TV shows and movies and help you keep track of them. It is an easy to use application that follows the Google Android Guidelines.

Some of it's top features are:

  • Maintaining a list of your favorite TV shows.
  • Maintaining a list of your favorite movies.
  • Keeping track of your favorite TV shows.
  • Discover new and popular TV shows.
  • See what your friends and family are watching.
  • May or may not help you win wars.

Installation

My Watchlist is available in the Google Play Store. it requires Android 4.0 or higher.

You can download My Watchlist to your device using the Google Play Store app or your computer (play.google.com).

Using the Google Play Store app

  1. Click on this link.
  2. Open it by clicking on the Google Play Store icon.
  3. Touch Install.
  4. Touch Accept after reviewing the app's permissions.
  5. My Watchlist is now installed on your device.

Using your computer (play.google.com)

  1. Click on this link.
  2. Click Install.
  3. Review the app's permissions, choose your device from the drop-down menu, and click Install.
  4. My Watchlist will be installed on your chosen device shortly.

Note: Android apps are intended for supported Android devices and can’t be used on your computer. If you don't have the Google Play Store app, visit Google's list of supported devices to see if your device is listed.
